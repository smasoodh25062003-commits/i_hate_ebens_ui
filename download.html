Installation steps
1
Unzip the downloaded file
Extract ebens-extension.zip to a permanent folder on your machine — somewhere it won't get moved or deleted.
2
Open Chrome Extensions
In Chrome, navigate to chrome://extensions and toggle on Developer mode in the top-right corner.
3
Load the unpacked extension
Click "Load unpacked", then select the ebens-extension folder you just extracted.
4
Pin & launch on e‑Benefits
Pin the extension icon in your Chrome toolbar. Open the e‑Benefits portal, click the icon, then hit "Launch Scheduler".
Tip: keep the extension folder in a stable location. If you move or delete it, Chrome will show an error and you'll need to reload it from chrome://extensions.
